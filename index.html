Shimla Tour

2 Nights / 3 Days. Experience the Queen of Hills with colonial charm, scenic views, and Kufri adventures.

Day 1: Delhi – Shimla
Arrive Delhi, evening Volvo bus to Shimla (overnight journey).

Day 2: Shimla Local Sightseeing
Visit Indian Institute of Advanced Study, Sankat Mochan Temple, Vaishno Gufa, Jakhoo Hills & Temple. Overnight stay.

Day 3: Kufri Excursion & Shimla
Kufri (Himalayan National Park, pony/yak rides, views). Shimla: Christ Church, Scandal Point, Mall Road shopping. Evening free. Overnight.

Day 4: Shimla – Delhi
Check-out, free day, evening Volvo back to Delhi. Tour ends.

Free time, evening Volvo to Delhi. Tour ends.
